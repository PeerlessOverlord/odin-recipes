Karaage

Description:

Japanese chicken dish, high in protein with moderately high amounts of carbs and fats. Is very crunchy, savoury and delicious

ingredients:

  • White flour
  • diced chicken breast or thigh meat
  • Lipids: butter or oil
  • Breading or crumbs
  • Soy sauce
  • eggs

Steps:

  1. prepare a batter from the flour and egg yolk and whites
  2. coat chicken in batter
  3. coat chicken in crumbs
  4. deep fry chicken in lipids
  5. serve with soy sauce and other desirable extra condiments
