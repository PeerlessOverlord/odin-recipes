Nachos

Description:

Mexican dish that is high in all three macro nutrients and micronutrients, very nutritionous, has many textures and is incredibly delicious and can be very satiating.

ingredients:

  • tortilla chips
  • beef mince
  • Lipids: butter or oil
  • spices
  • tomatoes
  • red onions
  • jalapenos
  • cheese
  • lemon juice
  • guacamole
  • sour cream or yoghurt

Steps:

  1. stir fry beef mince in lipids
  2. dice tomatoes, onions and jalapenos
  3. in a bowl, place beef mince, diced vegetables and tortilla chips
  4. top it off with melting cheese, guacamole and sour cream or yoghurt
  5. garnish with lemons
