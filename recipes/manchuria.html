Manchuria

Description:

This is cabbage veg manchruia, a spicy and savoury dish high in carbs and fats, but not so much in protein. It is incredibly tasty, but be warned: excess consumption can cause horrible constipation.

ingredients:

  • White flour
  • Shredded Cabbage
  • Lipids: butter or oil
  • spices
  • tomato sauce
  • soy sauce
  • water

Steps:

  1. Make The balls
  2. Prepare the oil
  3. Fry the balls in the oil
  4. removing the balls, bloom the spices
  5. add some flour and water
  6. add the two sauces
  7. stir the sauce and pour it over the fried cabbage balls.
